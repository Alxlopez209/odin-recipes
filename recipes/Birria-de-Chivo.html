Birria de Chivo

A bowl of Birria de Chivo

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo of Chivo meat
  • 4 liters of water
  • 1/2 of a whole onion
  • 3 skinless garlic cloves
  • 300 grams of cooked chile guajillo
  • 1 tbspn of dried blended comino
  • 7 dry laurel leaves
  • 1/2 tbspn of grated ginger
  • 1/2 of whole onion thinly diced
  • 1 tbspn of thime
  • salt and pepper as desired

Steps

  1. Cook the chivo meat in a big pot with water and add the half oinion and garlic cloves for approximately 180 minutes (2 hours 30 minutes)
  2. Meanwhile, prepare a blend stew of the chile guajillo with onion, comino, laurel, ginger, thime, salt and pepper. Blend and drain
  3. Incorporate this stew to the meat once it's done cooking and leave it on medium heat for 60 minutes more so it can add flavor
  4. Serve
